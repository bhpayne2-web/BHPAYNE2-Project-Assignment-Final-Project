Classic Paris for First-Time Visitors

This Paris itinerary is designed for first-time visitors who want iconic landmarks, museums, cafes, shopping, and time to enjoy the city atmosphere.

Back to Home

Daily Itinerary

Day 1: Eiffel Tower and Seine Walk

Start with the Eiffel Tower area, then walk along the Seine. Choose a cafe dinner and enjoy the city lights in the evening.

Day 2: Louvre and Tuileries

Visit the Louvre in the morning, then walk through the Tuileries Garden. Explore nearby shops and cafes in the afternoon.

Day 3: Montmartre and Local Cafes

Spend the day in Montmartre, visit Sacre-Coeur, and enjoy smaller streets, bakeries, and local restaurants.

Day 4: Shopping and Final Views

Use the final day for shopping, favorite photo stops, and a relaxed meal before leaving Paris.

Trip Tips

  • Buy museum tickets ahead of time when possible.
  • Leave extra time for walking between neighborhoods.
  • Keep one evening open for an unplanned cafe or viewpoint.
